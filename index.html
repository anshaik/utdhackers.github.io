UTD Hackers

We create, innovate, and build software and hardware projects at hackathons.

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where people build technology ranging from apps to other kinds of software. They are very fast paced and are great learning experiences.

Why should I go?

You can meet many prospective employers, and it looks very good on your resume. You also get free food, (usually) free travel, and get a lot of connections with other talented programmers!
